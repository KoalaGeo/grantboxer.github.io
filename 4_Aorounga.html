AOROUNGA CRATER - CHAD


Crater Name(s) Aorounga Crater
Country Chad
Longitude 19 deg 15 min E
Latitude 20 deg 15 min N
Diameter (km) 16 km
Age <345 Ma
Impact Origin Evidence PDF and Shatter Cones
Description The impact structure comprises a 9 km wide structurally complex central zone, surrounded by a 2-3 km zone of low relief, followed by and outer zone some 3.5km in width.
Landsat Scene Path 182 Row 47
References Reimold W U and Koeberl C. 2014. Impact Structures in Africa: A Review. Journal of African Earth Sciences 93 (2014), 57-175.
Date Data Entered 2014 09 15
